3 Funny jokes

1. Snail with an attitude A guy is sitting at home when he hears a knock at the door. He opens the door and sees a snail on the porch. He picks up the snail and throws it as far as he can. Three years later there’s a knock on the door. He opens it and sees the same snail. The snail says: ‘What the hell was that all about?’ - Nelson Mandela

2. A genie and an idiot Three guys stranded on a desert island find a magic lantern containing a genie, who grants them each one wish. The first guy wishes he was off the island and back home. The second guy wishes the same. The third guy says: ‘I’m lonely. I wish my friends were back here.’ - Walt Disney

3 Off to work A guy shows up late for work. The boss yells, You shouldve been here at 8.30! He replies. Why? What happened at 8.30? - Steve Jobs
